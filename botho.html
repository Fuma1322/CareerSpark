Institute Overview

Established in 1997, Botho University (BU) has quickly emerged as a leading multi-disciplinary tertiary education provider in Africa, with campuses across Lesotho, Botswana, Eswatini, Namibia, and Ghana, as well as an Online Learning Campus. The university's commitment to quality education is evident in it's rigorous approval processes; all programs are sanctioned by the relevant regulatory bodies in each country of operation, including the Council of Higher Education (CHE) in Lesotho. As part of it's ongoing expansion, Botho University is nearing completion of a state-of-the-art campus in Lesotho, expected to open in early 2025. This campus will further enhance the university's capacity to deliver qualifications ranging from certificates to master's degrees, with plans for doctoral programs on the horizon.

Currently home to over 4,500 students and nearly 300 staff members, Botho University offers academic programs through three key faculties: the Faculty of Business & Accounting, the Faculty of Engineering and Technology, and the Faculty of Health and Education. The university's strategic plan for 2023-2027 emphasizes Vibrance, Innovation, and Impact, positioning Botho University as a dynamic institution dedicated to fostering a vibrant educational experience. Core values of integrity, excellence, and inclusiveness drive the university's mission to produce well-rounded, entrepreneurial graduates who are equipped to thrive in the global job market and contribute to sustainable economic growth.

Botho University takes pride in its commitment to quality, having achieved BOS ISO 9001:2015 certification, which aligns its operations with international standards for quality assurance. This reputation for excellence ensures that graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the workforce and are instrumental in transforming the economy. The university's vision is to be the institution of choice, offering an innovative, digitally-enabled education that fosters a vibrant community of students, staff, and alumni dedicated to driving sustainable development and making a significant impact in Africa.

Through it's mission, Botho University aims to deliver an educational experience that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and competencies necessary for success in the evolving world of work. The university is committed to stakeholder satisfaction, continuous improvement, and environmental sustainability across all its endeavors, including leadership, curriculum development, teaching, research, and community engagement. With its focus on producing industry-ready graduates, Botho University continues to play a pivotal role in advancing education and driving productivity across the African continent.
